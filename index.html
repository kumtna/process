Official MTNA Collegiate Membership Application Process

1

Click the link below Go to MTNA Collegiate Membership Site then Click “Join Now”

2

Fill in personal information and click “Save”

3

Scroll down and find the Collegiate Membership (Student)

4

Continue filling in personal information and save

5

Scroll down and click “Next”

6

Select “Univ Of Kansas” in the school list and click “Next”

7

Select “None” for professional groups/affiliations and click “Next”

8

Confirm your information

9

Complete membership fee payment in the cart
